# Regulatory Traceability

Traceability matrices linking business rules to applicable regulations and originating COBOL programs.

## Regulatory Frameworks in Scope

| Framework | Scope |
|-----------|-------|
| FSA (FFFS 2014:5) | Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority regulations |
| PSD2 | Payment Services Directive — Strong Customer Authentication |
| GDPR | General Data Protection Regulation — customer data handling |
| AML/KYC | Anti-Money Laundering / Know Your Customer |
| DORA | Digital Operational Resilience Act |
| EBA Outsourcing | EBA Guidelines on Outsourcing Arrangements |

## Status

Traceability matrices will be populated as business rules are extracted and mapped to their applicable regulations. Each business rule document includes a regulatory mapping section linking the rule to specific regulation articles.
